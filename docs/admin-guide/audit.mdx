## User

The **User** tab displays valuable information about specific users.
In the user tab, select a particular user to view details such as a list of **Accessible reports**, list of **Removed reports** or reports revoked from the BI platform for that user and the list of reports **Frequently accessed** by the user.

## Reports

The Reports tab displays report details such as: **Report name, Agent Name, No. of users** who have accessed that Report, **No. of Views** for that Report and **No. of Favorites** assigned to the specific Report:

  <div class="center">
    <Zoom>
      <img alt="Audit - Reports tab" src={useBaseUrl('doc-images/admin-guide/audit-reports.png')}/>
    </Zoom>
    <p>Audit Reports</p>
  </div>

* You can filter the reports based on **Report name** and **Agent name**.
* You can also sort the reports based on **No. of users, No. of Views**, and **No. of Favorites**.

Select any report name to view detailed information such as **User Name**(s) who accessed the report, **Created(ion) Date** of the user and the **Last viewed on** timestamp on the report:
